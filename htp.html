Welcome to the Jeopardy board game! In this exciting trivia game, your goal is to answer questions and accumulate points to become the ultimate Jeopardy champion. Here's how to play:
Players take turns selecting a category and point value from the game board. The amount shown is how many points the winner wil recieve

Once a question is read, players have a limited time to buzz in or raise their hand to answer. The first player to do so gets the chance to respond. Use a buzzer or a designated method for signaling your readiness.

Finaly the palyer that signals first will type the question into the field .If the player answers correctly, they earn the points associated with the question. If they answer incorrectly, they lose those points, and other players have a chance to buzz in and answer. Continue taking turns, selecting questions, and accumulating points until all the questions on the game board are answered.
